About DIGICORP

We started DIGICORP because most marketing reports are written to impress, not to inform. We do the opposite.

Our story

Real strategy, not recycled templates

DIGICORP was built on a simple frustration — too many agencies sell the same playbook to every client and call it "strategy." A gym, a law firm, and a clothing brand don't grow the same way, and they shouldn't be marketed the same way either.

We work closely with each business to understand what's actually true about it — its strengths, its market, its real competition — before we plan a single post or campaign.
